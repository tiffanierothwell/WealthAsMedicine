For the next 150 years of Indigenous-led
ownership, sovereignty, and generational wealth.

For 150 years, Indigenous Peoples have been excluded from financial systems never built for them. Wealth as Medicine is a reframing of what wealth means, what it can build, and who it has always belonged to.

Mike David, Kanien'kehá:ka, and Michelle Bryant-Gravelle, Ts’msyen from Lax Kw’alaams, are building that future from the inside, through the co-founding of Little Tree Capital, and now through this book. Their invitation to Indigenous readers: see finance not just as money, but as reconciliation, sovereignty, and protection for generations to come.

“Wealth among traditional people is measured by having enough to give away.” — Robin Wall Kimmerer, Braiding Sweetgrass
